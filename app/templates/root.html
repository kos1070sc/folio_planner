Folio Planner — Quick Start Guide

Welcome. This guide will show you how to use the Folio Planner, step by step. With the planner, you can:

  • Create your folio
  • Choose a theme
  • Upload reference images
  • Keep track of deadlines

    • Follow the steps below to get started.

    1Create your folio

    Start by creating a new folio.

    1. Click on Create New in the navigation bar.
    2. Enter your folio theme — this is the main idea or concept for your work.
    3. Click Submit to save your folio and begin planning.

    Once your folio is created, you'll move on to uploading your reference images.

    2Upload reference images

    Reference images help you visualise your paintings on the folio.

    1. Open your folio.
    2. Click Upload Image next to the painting you’re planning.
    3. Select an image from your device.
    4. Click Submit to save the image to your folio.

    You can update or replace images at any time.

    3Keep track of dealines

    Your folio automatically shows the term and week each painting is due.

  • See upcoming deadlines
  • Stay on track with your work
  • Avoid last-minute stress

    • Planning ahead makes your folio more manageable.
